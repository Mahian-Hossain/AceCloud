BOperating principles

How we behave when no one is watching.

01
Senior by default.
Partners do the work. No layered juniors. No PMs translating between disciplines. The person in the kickoff is the person on the deliverable.
02
Written before drawn.
Every gate is a written memo. If we can't argue the strategy on paper, we don't deserve to argue it in pixels.
03
Built to compound.
We hand back systems — design libraries, growth infra, dashboards. Not a one-shot site that ages in a year.
04
Quiet by design.
No awards-bait. No Twitter performance. Our clients are reference customers. That's the only marketing we do.

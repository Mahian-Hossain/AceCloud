The challenge

Atlas Foundry had been in business since 1972. Their reputation was impeccable in the industry — word of mouth and trade show relationships kept them booked. But a new generation of procurement engineers was searching Google first, and Atlas wasn't showing up.

A technical audit revealed the core problem: their site had critical crawling issues, zero structured data, no internal linking architecture and a page speed score of 23. Google couldn't understand what they made or who they served.

Our approach

We rebuilt the site from scratch on Next.js with Contentful CMS, then implemented a programmatic SEO strategy built around 400+ auto-generated landing pages targeting specific alloy + application combinations ("aerospace-grade titanium castings for turbine housings" type queries).

Each page was built from a structured data model in Contentful — alloy type, industry vertical, specification, tolerance class — so the marketing team could generate new landing pages without developer involvement. The content itself was written to technical buyer standards, not watered down for SEO.

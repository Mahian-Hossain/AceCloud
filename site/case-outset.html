The challenge

Outset Capital's founders had impeccable credentials — ex-operators from Figma, Stripe and Notion, plus a thesis they'd refined over a decade of angel investing. But their digital presence was a two-line placeholder. Every founder who Googled them hit a dead end.

In early-stage VC, the site functions as social proof before the meeting. It tells a founder: this fund is serious, organised, and worth their time. Outset's placeholder was actively undermining their outreach before conversations even started.

Our approach

We started with a positioning intensive — two days with the founding partners to distil their thesis into something a founder could absorb in thirty seconds. The phrase we landed on: "first cheque, high conviction." Every element of the brand and site flows from that sentence.

The visual direction is deliberately editorial: clean white with an Instrument Serif headline voice for gravitas, Geist for data and navigation, and a near-black primary that reads as serious without being corporate. The portfolio section was built as a filterable grid with custom data on each investment.

Twelve weeks. Brand system, copywriting, 8-page site, CMS for portfolio companies and insights, and a custom fund metrics dashboard for the team.

"Every founder we pitched Googled us first. Before the rebrand, what they found undermined the meeting before it started. Now the site does half the work for us — it arrives before we do."

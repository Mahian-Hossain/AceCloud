Questions

Honest answers.

How do you work alongside our existing product team?

We integrate into your workflow — standups, sprint planning, Jira or Linear, whatever you're using. We act like a pod, not a vendor. You get senior design and engineering capacity without a full-time hire. We typically embed for a minimum of three months.

Can you build on our existing codebase?

Yes. We do a codebase audit first and document the component library we'll build on top of. If the existing code is in rough shape, we'll tell you honestly and scope a cleanup alongside the new work.

Do you do UX research?

Yes — user interviews, usability testing, session recording analysis and quantitative funnel analysis. For internal tools, we run workflow interviews with the actual users (usually your ops or sales team) before we design anything.

Tell us which surface is costing you the most.

We'll come back with a scope and a point of view within 48 hours. No pitch required.

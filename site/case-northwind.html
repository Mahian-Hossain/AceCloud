The challenge

Northwind's product had earned a devoted following among staff engineers at Series B and C companies. The founders knew they needed to raise but their site was generating friction at exactly the wrong moment in the investor journey.

The brief: build a site that feels like the product — precise, powerful and senior-by-default. Not another "we make devops easy" SaaS template with a screenshot carousel.

Our approach

We designed the site around a single tension: infrastructure that feels effortless. The visual language draws from terminal UI and industrial print — monospace type for commands, generous white space for clarity, and a single editorial illustration style for concept visualisation.

Performance was non-negotiable. The site needed to be an example of what Northwind enables. 97 Lighthouse average at launch, ISR for all content pages, and a Sanity CMS their marketing team picked up in two hours.

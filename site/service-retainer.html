How it works

Less overhead. More output.

We integrate into your workflow like a senior hire — just without the recruitment, onboarding, benefits and notice period.

Week 1 · Onboarding

We review your existing design system, codebase, analytics setup and roadmap. We attend standups, get access to your tools (Figma, Linear, GitHub, Slack) and agree on a sprint cadence that fits your team's rhythm.

Ongoing · Sprints

Two-week design and engineering cycles with a written brief at the start of every sprint. Weekly demo on Fridays. We ship behind a feature flag so you can review before anything goes to production.

Monthly · Review

End-of-month review covering output, metrics movement, design system health and the next quarter's priorities. We also run a retrospective — what we shipped, what we'd do differently and what we're doubling down on.

Quarterly · Strategy

A written strategic review of your digital presence — brand consistency, site performance, growth metrics and roadmap alignment. Includes recommendations for the next quarter and a prioritised backlog of high-leverage work.

Questions

Straight answers on retainers.

What's the minimum commitment?

Three months. This gives us enough time to onboard, understand your codebase and brand system, ship meaningful work and demonstrate results. Month-to-month arrangements are available after the initial three-month term at a 15% premium.

What if we don't have enough work one month?

Retainer hours roll forward one month. If you're consistently under-utilising the pod, we'll have an honest conversation about whether you need a smaller tier or a different engagement model. We'd rather have you on the right plan than paying for capacity you're not using.

Can we pause or cancel?

30 days notice to pause or cancel after the initial three-month term. No penalty, no hard feelings. We hand over everything — code, Figma files, documentation — in a structured offboarding session.

We have an in-house designer. Can we still use a retainer?

Absolutely. Many of our retainer clients have in-house designers. We typically handle the more complex engineering work, growth infrastructure and overflow design capacity so your in-house team can focus on core product. We integrate alongside your team, not on top of it.

How many retainer clients do you take on?

A maximum of four active retainer clients at any time. This is a hard limit — it's what keeps the senior people in the work. If we're at capacity we'll tell you directly and offer to put you on a waitlist.
